How It Works

From Donor History
to Prediction in Seconds

Our model uses XGBoost — analyzing recency, frequency, monetary value, time, and total count of blood donations to determine likelihood of future donation.

01
Input Donor Data

Enter the donor's recency (months since last donation), frequency (total donations), total blood volume donated, and time since first donation.

02
ML Prediction

The system applies RFMTC transformations and normalizes the input, preparing it for the trained classification model with high-dimensional feature mapping.

03
Smart Matching System

A trained machine learning classifier optimized on blood donation data produces a matching score of a donor with a matching receiver.

04
Ethically Designed

Blood banks receive a clear Yes/No prediction with confidence score, enabling smarter outreach campaigns and better inventory management.

About the Project

Why Blood Donation
Prediction Matters

Blood shortages affect millions of surgeries, trauma cases, and cancer treatments every year. By predicting donor behavior, hospitals can proactively engage likely donors before supply drops critically low.

Interpretable Results

Each prediction comes with feature importance explanations so medical staff understand the reasoning, not just the output.

Ethically Designed

Privacy-preserving by design — no personally identifiable information is required. Predictions are based solely on behavioral donation patterns.
